MVP Outline:

Nav -
  Menu containing links to the pages in the mvp,
  present on every page.

Pages:
- Home page
      Explain basic functionality of website.
- Add Burger
      Takes user to page with form to fill in all details,
      then adds these to the burger_tracker db, and notifies
      of success or errors.
- View
    - Takes user to page where they can select available
      eateries and view all details relating to that eatery.
    - Can also select by day, and show all details of all
      deals available on that day.
